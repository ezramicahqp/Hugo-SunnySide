Ballerina Shoes

Ballerina Shoes 2021-03-16

Ballerina Shoes was a school requirement. It was fun because painting is not a daily thing or particularly a hobby of mine, it was also exciting because we used coffee pigments as our colors. It was pretty challenging because I have to find out how will I get various shades of the coffee by measuring how much water will I use in diluting it. As the shade goes darker, lesser water is added so the texture adds challenge when painting. I did lots of trial and error but in the end, I was happy with the result.

Tippy Toes

Tippy Toes 2024-01-21

Tippy Toes Is another subject requirement. I don’t really paint unless it is necessary like for example, a school requirement, partly because it is costly but the main reason is that I’m not really drawn to it and it takes so much time to accomplish. My life/house situation just don’t fit to it. This painting involves a previous theme which are ballet shoes, maybe I really do have a thing for ballet, I just find ballerinas awesome. I like how they dance and what they do. In fact, if I will be given the chance to do ballet, I would gladly try it though I don’t think I would do good.
